Tomato Salad

What Is Tomato Salad

Tomato Salad is an easy and fun to make dish for chefs of any skill level. All it takes is a few simple ingredients and the ability to cut up veggies and voila, you're done. I make this dish at ever one of my family cookouts and it's a hit every time!

Follow these simple steps and you too can wow your friends and family without breaking a sweat. Think you have what it takes. Read on for a full breakdown of this delectable dish!

The All Important Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Water
  • 1/4 Cup Vegatble Oil
  • 1/4 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Teaspoon Freshly Ground Pepper
  • 3 Cucumbers
  • 3 Tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 1/2 Cup Distilled White Vinegar

The Steps

  1. Peel and slice cucumbers to 1/4 thickness
  2. Cut tomatoes into wedges
  3. Slice onion and seperate into rings
  4. Whisk water, vinegar, oil, sugar, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl until smooth. Then add cucumbers tomatoes and onion and stir to coat.
  5. Cover bowl with platic wrap
  6. Refrigerate for at least two hours
