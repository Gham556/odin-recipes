Good Old Fashion Pancakes

What Makes My Pancakes So Good?

Everyone loves pancakes. You know it and I know it. So why should you choose to make my pancakes over anyone elses?

What makes my pancakes so good isn't that they were made with love. Although they are. What makes my pancakes so good is countless hours of honing this recipe to a science. Keep reading to learn how you can make my good old fashion pancakes!

The Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 3 and 1/2 Teaspoons Baking Powder
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Tablespoon White Sugar
  • 1 and 1/4 Cups Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 3 Tablespoons Melted Butter

How To Make Good Old Fashion Pancakes

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximatley 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown both sides and serve hot.
