Old Fashioned Lemonade

What Is Old Fashioned Lemonade

Old fashioned lemonade is a classic drink that anyone can enjoy. Your kids could even make it to sell to the neighbors. It is super easy and fun to make!

This is the perfect family recipe to use as a teaching moment. Or you could just serve it up on a hot day! Keep reading for more on how to make your own Old Fashioned Lemonade"

The Ingredients

  • 6 Lemons
  • 1 Cup White Sugar
  • 6 Cups Water

How It's Done

  1. Juice lemons till you have 1 cup juice
  2. Combine juice, sugar, and water in a 1/2 gallon pitcher. Stir until sugar resolves. Taste and add more water if desired.
  3. Chill and serve over ice
